Explore

Explore the unseen

Never stop exploring. More you explore, more you learn. The world is yours to explore.

We started exploring project we can really work on that can maintain interest of every group member till the end and we come up with real time sudoku solver where one can scan sudoku, and get its solution in seconds. We maked it user-interactive by allowing user to solve that sudoku with added functionalities like hint, reset and clear specific cell. We extended the project by adding random sudoku board where user can simply solve the various sudoku and illustrated how actually backtracking works to take out solution of sudoku
